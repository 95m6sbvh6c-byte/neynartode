# Leaderboard Page Guide

> Vote on hosts and compete for Season prizes

---

## Overview

The Leaderboard shows the top contest hosts ranked by their total score. Vote to support your favorite hosts and influence who wins the Season prize pool!

---

## Season Stats

### Season Countdown
- Shows time remaining in current season
- When season ends, top hosts win the prize pool

### Host Prize Pool
- ETH accumulated for contest creators
- Top hosts split this pool at season end
- Grows from voting fees and contest activity

### All-Time Burned
- Total NEYNARTODES tokens permanently removed
- Burns come from voting (500 per vote)
- Deflationary mechanism

### Current Leader
- Click to visit their Warpcast profile
- Shows their current point total

## Voting System

### Your Voting Power

| Stat | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Votes Left** | Remaining votes for today (max 10) |
| **Cost** | 1000 NEYNARTODES per vote |
| **Reset** | Daily at midnight UTC |

### How Voting Works

```
1 Vote = 1000 $NEYNARTODES
              |
        +-----+-----+
        |           |
       500         500
      BURNED     TREASURY
        |           |
        v           v
   Reduces      Grows
   Supply     Prize Pool
```

### Vote Impact

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Upvote** | +5,000 points to host |
| **Downvote** | -5,000 points from host |

## How to Vote

1. **Find a host** you want to support (or oppose)
2. **Check your votes remaining** (top of page)
3. **Click the vote button** (thumbs up or down)
4. **Confirm transaction** in your wallet
5. **Wait for confirmation** (~15-30 seconds)
6. **See score update** immediately after

## Feed the Neynartodes

When Clanker fees are available, you'll see the "Feed the Neynartodes" button:

**What it does:**
1. Collects accumulated Clanker creator fees
2. Splits 50/50 between treasury and recipient
3. Triggers Captain Hook to compound liquidity
4. Anyone can call this (costs gas)

**Benefits:**
- Grows the prize pool
- Strengthens token liquidity
- Rewards active community members

## Troubleshooting

**"Daily vote limit reached"**
- You've used all 10 votes today
- Resets at midnight UTC
- Come back tomorrow!

**"Already voted for this host"**
- You can only vote once per host per day
- Try voting for other hosts

**"Not enough balance"**
- Need 1000 NEYNARTODES per vote
- Plus small ETH for gas

**"Not whitelisted"**
- Season 0 Beta requires whitelist
- Only whitelisted addresses can vote

**Vote not showing**
- Transactions take 15-30 seconds
- Check BaseScan if unsure
- Refresh the page after confirmation
